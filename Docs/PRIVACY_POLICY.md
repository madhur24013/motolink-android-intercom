# MotoLink Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

MotoLink is a rider/pillion intercom app. This app does not require account sign-in and does not run a central user-profile backend.

## Data MotoLink Uses

- Bluetooth permissions:
  Needed to discover and connect paired MotoLink phones.
- Microphone:
  Needed for intercom voice calls.
- Network access:
  Needed for WebRTC signaling and media relay (TURN/STUN) when internet path is used.
- Local storage:
  Used to save app settings, paired-device info, and local logs on your phone.

## Data Sharing

- MotoLink does not sell personal data.
- Voice traffic is peer-to-peer/WebRTC path between paired devices and may use configured TURN relay infrastructure.
- Support requests sent by email are handled by the support contact listed in the app.

## Data Retention

- Pairing info, settings, and logs are stored locally on the device until removed by the user or app uninstall.

## Security Notes

- Pairing uses MotoLink identity validation between devices.
- If trust validation fails, MotoLink requires re-pairing before call signaling continues.
